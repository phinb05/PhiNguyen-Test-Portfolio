About me

About Bang Phi Nguyen

In high school I was the kid cold-calling companies for venue sponsorships. At VinUniversity, I turned that into $29K raised across 25+ corporate partners. At Stavian, I built lead databases for European expansion. The work changed, but the pattern didn't: behind every team doing good work, there was a broken system slowing them down.

An exchange semester in Germany taught me something the spreadsheets never could. I watched people work efficiently, leave on time, and still outperform. It wasn't about doing more. It was about making sure the right person does the right thing. That question, "What is actually worth my 100%?", led me to automation.

RAG Customer Support

The Pain

An English language center in Vietnam answers the same customer questions hundreds of times. Staff spend hours on repetitive replies instead of teaching.

The Build

Business documents are chunked and embedded into a vector store. When a customer asks a question, RAG retrieves relevant context and an LLM generates a grounded answer with source citations.

B2B Lead Generation

The Pain

A salesman copies lead data row by row for 3+ hours a day. Not because he's bad at his job. Because the system is. Manual sourcing across multiple regions with no deduplication.

The Build

n8n scrapes leads from multiple online sources, runs multi-state deduplication against existing contacts, enriches data, and pushes qualified leads directly to the CRM.

The Result

3hrs+

of manual data entry eliminated per session
